amoreywebdesign/ concept manifesto

amoreywebdesign is the studio of artist alice morey.

Born from a desire to merge the tactile, experimental nature of artistic practice with the logic and structure of the web, this project is not just a pivot—it's a creative expansion.

Rooted in my experience as a contemporary artist, this studio is an extension of how I engage with the world: through experimentation, autonomy, and craft.

amoreywebdesign creates clean, beautiful, and functional websites for creatives, institutions, and small businesses—without bloated tech or a corporate edge.

This isn’t about reinventing the wheel, but about approaching web design as a new artistic medium—websites as small artworks, made with care, for humans, not algorithms. The idea emerged during a time of uncertainty: navigating career shifts, job markets, and self-doubt. And in that space, I realised: I could build something of my own.

Now I’m building sites from scratch, learning frameworks, integrating tools like Stripe, and embracing code as part of my practice. This is a brand made from experience, from limitations turned into strengths, and a desire to help others have a home online that feels personal, accessible, and thoughtfully built.

If you're someone who values intention, transparency, and design with a soul—this is for you.
