CONCEPT

** amoreywebdesign is the web design studio of artist alice morey.

** born from a desire to merge the tactile, experimental nature of artistic practice with the logic and structure of the web, this project is not just a pivot—it's a creative expansion.

** rooted in my experience as a contemporary artist, this studio is an extension of how I engage with the world: through experimentation, autonomy, and craft.

** amoreywebdesign creates clean, beautiful, and functional websites for creatives, institutions, and small businesses—without bloated tech or a corporate edge.

** this isn’t about reinventing things, but about approaching web design as a new artistic medium—websites as small artworks, made with care, for humans, not algorithms. The idea emerged during a time of uncertainty: navigating career shifts, job markets, and self-doubt. And in that space, I realised: I could build something of my own.

** now I’m building sites from scratch, learning frameworks, integrating tools like stripe, and embracing code as part of my practice. this is a brand made from a developing experience, from limitations turned into strengths, and a desire to help others have a home online that feels personal, accessible, and thoughtfully built.

If you're someone who values intention, transparency, and design with a diy twist please get in touch!
